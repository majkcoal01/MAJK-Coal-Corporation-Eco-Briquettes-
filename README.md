# MAJK-Coal-Corporation-Eco-Briquettes-
An environmentally driven enterprise focused on producing sustainable, high-efficiency briquettes made from organic and biodegradable waste materials such as coconut shells, rice husks, sawdust, and agricultural residues.

## What We Do
We turn organic waste into affordable and clean fuel for households and small food businesses.

---

## Why Choose Us
- Lower cost than charcoal  
- Longer burning time  
- Less smoke, cleaner cooking  
- Environmentally sustainable  

---

## Target Market
Households, carinderias, BBQ stalls, and small food businesses.

---

## Sales & Marketing
We sell through markets, resellers, and online via Facebook, Messenger, and TikTok. Promotion is done through social media, flyers, and live demos.
